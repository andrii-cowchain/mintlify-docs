---
title: "Send an Invitation to a New Organization Team Member"
sidebarTitle: "Invite Member"
description: "POST /sdk/organization/team/invite — Invite a new team member by submitting their email address and desired role; the invitee receives an acceptance email."
openapi: post /sdk/organization/team/invite
---

Send an invitation to a new member to join the organization's team. Submit the invitee's email address and the desired role in the request body. The invited user will receive an email prompt to accept and join the organization.
