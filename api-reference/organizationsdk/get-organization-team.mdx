---
title: "List All Team Members Within the Current Organization"
sidebarTitle: "Get Team"
description: "GET /sdk/organization/team — Retrieve all team members in the authenticated user's organization, including their roles, member IDs, and wallet assignments."
openapi: get /sdk/organization/team
---

Retrieve a list of all team members belonging to the authenticated user's organization. The response includes each member's ID, role, email, and any wallets assigned to them within the organization.
