---
title: "Remove a Team Member from the Organization by Member ID"
sidebarTitle: "Remove Member"
description: "DELETE /sdk/organization/team/{memberId} — Remove a team member by their ID, revoking access to all associated organization wallets and resources."
openapi: delete /sdk/organization/team/{memberId}
---

Remove a team member from the organization by their unique member ID. Provide the member ID as a path parameter. This operation revokes the member's access to all organization wallets and resources; the user's account itself is not deleted.
