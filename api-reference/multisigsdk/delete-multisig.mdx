---
title: "Permanently Delete a Multisig Wallet Record by Its ID"
sidebarTitle: "Delete Multisig"
description: "DELETE /sdk/multisig/{id} — Permanently delete a multisig wallet record by its ID, removing the entry without affecting the underlying on-chain contract."
openapi: delete /sdk/multisig/{id}
---

Permanently delete a multisig wallet record from the Cowchain platform. Provide the multisig record ID as a path parameter. This operation removes the record from the platform but does not affect the underlying on-chain contract or wallet.
