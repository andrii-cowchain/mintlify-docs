---
title: "Get Multisig Wallet Details by Chain Type and Address"
sidebarTitle: "Get by Address"
description: "GET /sdk/multisig/id/{chainType}/{address} — Retrieve full details for a specific multisig wallet by its chain type and on-chain address."
openapi: get /sdk/multisig/id/{chainType}/{address}
---

Retrieve complete details for a specific multisig wallet by supplying its chain type and on-chain address. This endpoint returns the full multisig record, including configuration, signers, and status, for the matched wallet.
